💡 What is Carbon Sequestration?
Carbon sequestration is the process of capturing and storing atmospheric carbon dioxide. Activities like planting trees, practicing agroforestry, or improving soil carbon help offset your farm's emissions.

🎯 Tips for Calculating Sequestration
  • Trees: Mature trees sequester ~20-30 kg CO₂/year
  • Agroforestry: 5-10 ton CO₂/hectare/year
  • Soil Carbon: 0.5-2 ton CO₂/hectare/year
  • Composting: Reduces methane by ~0.5 kg CO₂e per kg composted
  • Update annually as your sequestration activities mature
