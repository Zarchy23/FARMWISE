Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are compatible?

FarmWise supports any IoT device that can connect via WiFi or cellular network and provides an API for data transmission. We officially support sensors from major manufacturers.

Do I need special technical skills?

No! Our setup wizard guides you through the entire process. Most devices are plug-and-play and can be set up in minutes.

What's the data update frequency?

Most devices send data every 5-15 minutes. You can configure this based on your needs and battery life.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All device data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. We follow industry standards for IoT device security.
